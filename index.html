ABOUT US

We are an Innovation, Research and Development [I, R&D] Company. We are a knowledge (and a know-how) business.

Our business aims to ADD VALUE to Individuals(B2I), other businesses (B2B) and other institutions (B2Insts.). Once an individual, business or any institute does business with us as a brand, they become anew: they become better versions of themselves. We are good at what we do, our clients only pay us for value!

Through Innovation, we strive to make the world a better place, that is, a new world! We achieve this goal by constantly researching and our brand is one of the coolest brands in the higher education sector.

We came into existence during the early stages of our Founder’s career in Accountancy, on the 1 st of September 2014 – we are still counting.

We realised that the fruits of what we are truly passionate about as a brand are so sweet that one needs to nurture it for about a lifetime. We are passionate about Accountancy, Property, Education and Information Technology and we believe that the commercial benefits to those are enjoyable for a lifetime.
